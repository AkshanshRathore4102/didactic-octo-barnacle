Password Privacy

Introduction

Passwords are the first line of defense against unauthorized access to your accounts. Protecting them is crucial to prevent identity theft, fraud, and data breaches.

Tips for Strong Passwords

  • Use unique passwords for every account.
  • Create passwords of at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid easily guessable information like birthdays or names.
  • Use a password manager to store and generate secure passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
